Publications

Publication Details

The International Conference on “Synergy in Mathematics: Advances in Fractional Calculus and Engineering Applications” offers authors of accepted and presented papers the opportunity to publish their work, subject to the conference review process, editorial screening, and the policies of the respective publication outlets.

All accepted papers that are presented at the conference will be eligible for consideration for publication in conference proceedings and/or special issues of reputed journals, depending on the quality, scope, and recommendations of the review and editorial committees. Potential publication avenues may include journals or proceedings indexed in UGC-CARE, Scopus, ESCI, SCIE, SCI, and Web of Science, subject to final approval, editorial acceptance, and the norms of the concerned publisher or journal.

Publication Consideration Conditions

Publication consideration will be based on the following conditions:

  • The paper must fall within the scope and themes of the conference;
  • The paper must be original, unpublished, and not under consideration elsewhere;
  • The paper must successfully pass the peer-review process and satisfy the quality standards of the conference and publication partner;
  • At least one author must register for the conference and present the paper;
  • The manuscript must be prepared in accordance with the conference template, ethical guidelines, and final submission requirements.

Review Process & Publication Decisions

The conference review process is intended to ensure academic quality, originality, relevance, and technical soundness. Submission of a paper does not automatically guarantee publication.

Final publication decisions will depend on reviewer comments, editorial recommendations, compliance with formatting and revision requirements, and the acceptance policy of the respective proceedings volume or journal special issue.

Charges & Additional Information

Authors are advised to note that Article Processing Charges (APC), publication fees, or page charges, if applicable under the selected journal/proceedings publisher, shall be borne by the author(s) as per the rules of the respective publication outlet. Detailed information regarding publication partners, indexing status, submission of revised manuscripts, and any applicable charges will be communicated through the conference website or official notification in due course.

The Organizing Committee is committed to facilitating high-quality publication opportunities for selected contributions and to promoting impactful research at the intersection of mathematics, fractional calculus, and engineering applications.
